OPTIMIZING SUPPLY CHAIN SOLUTIONS THROUGH FLEXIBILITY

In this fast paced environment, supply chain disruptions can mean missed sales opportunities. Our distribution centers, cross-docks, and fulfillment centers are often the last touch before your products reach the store shelves or customer’s hands, and we treat your business as if it were our own.

Creativity and flexibility are core to the NFI culture. By offering both contract and public warehousing, we can create solutions to optimize your supply chain. Our distribution services range from fully customized, dedicated, built-to-suit distribution centers to public warehouses that meet your smaller, shorter term requirements for flex space. Our in-house real estate team has intimate knowledge of key markets and works with our engineers to help our customers design their networks.

Our full suite of value-added services creates synergies for our customers, allowing you to focus on your core capabilities. You will get the best inventory and labor management available through our Tier 1 WMS systems.

Whether it’s storage in our over 50 million square feet of distribution space or constructing a new facility, contact us to have NFI find efficiencies in your supply chain so you can focus on what you do best.
